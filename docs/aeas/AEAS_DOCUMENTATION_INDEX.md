## üß† Key Concepts Explained

### The "Is" Mandate
**Definition**: Systems must present economic reality *as it is*, not as someone wants you to *act*.

**Compliant**: "The frequency is rising because price momentum increased 15%"  
**Prohibited**: "You should buy now based on this signal"

### Uncertainty Resonance (v1.1)
**Definition**: Data uncertainty is mapped to sensory "fuzziness"‚Äîstatic in audio, tremor in haptics, blurred edges in visuals.

**Why**: Truth includes doubt. Users must feel confident vs uncertain signals.

### Onboarding Resonance (v1.1)
**Definition**: Progressive multi-sensory training‚Äîstart with one sense, add more over 4 weeks.

**Why**: Prevents cognitive overload. Builds sensory literacy like learning a musical instrument.

### M3 McCrea Market Metrics
**Definition**: Reference implementation of AEAS‚Äîtranslates Avalanche blockchain data into 4 proprietary scores:

1. **Whale_Splash** (0-100): Large transaction activity ‚Üí Sub-bass (20-60 Hz)
2. **Tax_Axe** (0-100): Regulatory pressure ‚Üí Grinding timbre
3. **Trumpet_Dumpet** (0-100): Sell pressure ‚Üí Descending trumpet
4. **Market_Melee** (Character): Market personality (Racer/Bruiser/Sniper)

### Privacy-First AI Architecture (v1.1) üîê
**Definition**: Zero-retention, zero-training AI layer for economic cognition.

**Reference Implementation**: Venice AI
- **No data retention**: Queries processed and immediately discarded
- **No model training**: User data never improves the model
- **No third-party sharing**: Economic data remains completely private

**Strategic Claim**: "RangisNet processes your financial reality through a private AI layer that cannot leak, sell, or remember your data. Your economic intuition remains yours alone."

**Why it matters**: Financial data is the ultimate private asset. Privacy-preserving AI is existential for trust.
