# Harmonic Market Pulse (HMP)
_The Real-Time Heartbeat of Rangi’s Market Universe_

The Harmonic Market Pulse is a continuously generated rhythmic signal
summarizing market-wide conditions.

Think of it as:
- the “heartbeat”  
- the “breathing rate.”  
- the “mood”  

of the market.

---

## 1. Inputs

- global volatility  
- liquidity depth  
- volume flow  
- funding trend  
- cross-chain stability  

---

## 2. Pulse Types

| Pulse | Meaning |
|--------|----------|
| Steady | Healthy, trending |
| Flutter | High-risk, unstable |
| Spiking | Fast opportunity |
| Weak | Illiquidity, low conviction |
| Chaotic | Contagion or cascading moves |
