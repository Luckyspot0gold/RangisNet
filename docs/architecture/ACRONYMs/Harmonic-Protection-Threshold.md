# Harmonic Protection Thresholds (HPT)
_Protecting Users from Bad Transactions and Dangerous Conditions_

HPT defines thresholds where transactions become:
- unsafe  
- unwise  
- inefficient  
- at risk of loss  

When HPT triggers, the platform:
- warns  
- vibrates  
- flashes  
- or blocks  

---

## 1. Trigger Classes

### 1.1 Market Risk
Triggered by:
- extreme volatility
- liquidity collapse
- liquidation cascades

### 1.2 Network Risk
Triggered by:
- gas spikes
- mempool congestion
- validator instability

### 1.3 Bridge Risk
Triggered by:
- cross-chain stall
- high revert rate
- health score drop

---

## 2. HPT Signal Types

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Level 1 | soft audio cue |
| Level 2 | pulsating visual |
| Level 3 | disruptive vibration |
| Level 4 | freeze with override |
| Level 5 | total auto-stop |
